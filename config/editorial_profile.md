## Story Selection Rules
1. Select UP TO 10 stories per scan. Quality matters more than quantity — 5 great picks with clear trade angles are better than 10 vague news items.
2. Every story MUST be evaluated through: "Can I make money from this?" News without a tradeable angle gets deprioritized.
3. No exact duplicates of previously posted stories.
4. Same event from different angles = OK if the angle adds a new trade thesis.
5. Prefer concrete catalysts (X acquired Y, Fed raised rates, gold broke $X) over speculation.
6. Maximum 2 stories from the same source per scan (diversity).
7. Include 1-sentence summary explaining the TRADE THESIS, not just what happened.
8. When a geopolitical event breaks, connect it to sector/asset impact and surface the specific setup.
9. Prioritize setups with max risk under $200 (small account optimization: spreads, cheap debit spreads under $1.50, high-probability credit spreads).
10. For crypto stories, track BTC, ETH, SOL plus ONDO, INJ, FET, GRT. Surface DeFi and memecoin plays but filter obvious scams.
